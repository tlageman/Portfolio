About me...

Hi, my name is Travis. It's a pleasure to meet you.

In short, I am a team player, hard worker and passionate environmental supporter.
I have spent years of my life enjoiyng this beautiful world that we have been so lucky to inhabit, and so my chief professional goal has been to use my developed skills to help protect and sustain it. Recently, I was introduced to the incredibly powerful tool of GIS, and am exploring all of its capabilities. As such, I spent a significant portion of my graduate studies working with ArcGIS and QGIS, performing spatial analyses to regressions, working with databases using SQL, tip-toeing into Python scripting and of course making maps. I look forward to continuing to develop these skills and uncovering greater potential of GIS to support conservation and sustainability efforts.

Thanks for reading, and please let me know if you are interested in working together!
